Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The Church helps us to maintain organization, teachings, and to create a support system for members. By establishing a church, the Lord ensures that the correct doctrines are taught.



School Library

Was built in 1980

School libraries help students to get authentic information through the books written by reputed scholars who come from different parts of the world. A library plays an important role in creating a school culture, which helps every student to grow on their individual basis as well.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The building was built for plays, concerts, speeches, etc. It has been a key feature for the students and they enjoy it a lot.
